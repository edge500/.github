# Invite Message

Use this short message when inviting someone to join Off By One:

```text
Hey, I am building Off By One as a small open-source org for useful developer tools, local-first apps, AI experiments, and clean beginner-friendly projects.

I would like you to join as a contributor. No pressure to do big things immediately. Even docs, testing, ideas, and small fixes are valuable.

Org: https://github.com/off-byone
```

## Who To Invite

- People who already help in GitHub Discussions.
- Friends who can test projects honestly.
- Developers who like docs and small fixes.
- Designers who can make simple tools feel better.
