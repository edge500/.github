# Code Of Conduct

Off By One should be a calm place to build useful software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Give feedback that helps the work improve.
- Welcome beginners without lowering the quality bar.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Spam.
- Hate speech.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing discussions.

## Reporting

If something feels wrong, contact the organization owner through GitHub.

We will review reports and take action when needed, including removing comments, closing issues, or blocking users.
